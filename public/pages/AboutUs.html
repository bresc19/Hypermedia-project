About Us

Founded in Italy in 2000, today TopTech is a multinational group that focuses its consulting offering on hard technology challenges with large-scale business impact in data bases, security, IoT and machine learning.
TopTech started as a research spinoff at Politecnico of Milan and was incubated at its startup accelerator. Data and experimentation are part of our DNA.
We believe that science and engineering need to be seen as one. It is for this reason that we continuously focus on research, emerging ideas, and new technologies, routinely partnering with top academic and engineering research institutions.
In all we do, our goal is to build trusted relationships through client proximity, providing industry and technology expertise to help you meet the needs of your customers and citizens.
